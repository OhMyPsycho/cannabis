We Make a Green Change.

In GREEN HEADWAY INTERNATIONAL we are an organization and community dedicated to the research, planting, use of seeds, cultivation, and manufacturing of organic and medicinal products based on cannabis, using unique models of technology to promote organic self-cultivation in order to try to combat genetically modified crops, and that the world population has access to healthy and organic green food and products through decentralized crops.

GREEN HEADWAY INTERNATIONAL was founded in 2015 by a team of passionate farmers and cannabis researchers, among others. Our ideal is to revolutionize the new era of organic and cannabis cultivation by making organic and chemical-free products available worldwide, thanks to our disruptive agricultural technology, intelligent software and blockchain. By working towards this goal, our goal is to gradually fulfill the so-called concept of ORGANIC BY GREENGOLD ™. In addition to making the blockchain part of the technology to ensure that the product grown with GREENDOOR TECH ™ will be completely free of chemicals in 2020, ensuring its traceability from the seed. through the new technologies available today by implementing a new ecosystem of decentralized organics to bring quality products and services to our users. Educating them so that everyone can enter the new era of Blockchain Decentralized technology and organic gardening.
